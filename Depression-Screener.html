Depression Screener

We all experience highs and lows in life, moments of joy, and times when we feel sad or down.

Occasional sadness is normal, but if it persists, it could indicate depression.

Depression is one of the most common and misunderstood mental health conditions globally. According to the World Health Organization (WHO), it affects around 280 million people worldwide.

Depression can manifest differently in each person, with varying symptom intensity.

Y ou are not alone.  If you're experiencing depression symptoms, support is available to help you manage them.
